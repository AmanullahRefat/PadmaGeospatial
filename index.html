About Our Company

Padma Geospatial Solutions Ltd. is a leading consulting firm established in 2022, dedicated to delivering high-quality geospatial surveys and analytical services. We specialize in physical, human, and urban geography, leveraging cutting-edge GIS and remote sensing technologies to provide accurate, data-driven insights that empower decision-making across sectors.

Our strength lies in our agile and expert-driven approach. With a core team of three full-time professionals and a dynamic network of over 20 domain specialists, we assemble tailored project teams that combine precision, efficiency, and deep expertise. This flexible structure ensures that each project benefits from the right mix of skills and experience, whether it's a complex urban mapping initiative or a targeted environmental assessment.

At Padma Geospatial Solutions, we are committed to innovation, integrity, and impact. Our mission is to transform spatial data into actionable intelligence, helping clients navigate challenges and unlock opportunities through geospatial excellence.

Our Comprehensive Services

We offer a wide spectrum of geospatial services, from data acquisition and analysis to custom application development, tailored to meet the unique needs of your project.

Geographic Information Systems (GIS)

Our GIS services turn complex spatial data into clear, actionable intelligence. We help you visualize, question, analyze, and interpret data to understand relationships, patterns, and trends.

  • Spatial Analysis & Modeling
  • Geodatabase Design & Management
  • Network Analysis (Routing & Logistics)
  • Site Suitability Analysis
  • 3D GIS and Visualization
  • Asset Management & Tracking
  • Thematic & Cadastral Mapping
  • Data Conversion & Migration

Remote Sensing

We harness the power of satellite and aerial imagery to provide critical insights for environmental monitoring, resource management, and large-scale planning.

  • Satellite Image Processing & Analysis
  • Land Use/Land Cover (LULC) Classification
  • Change Detection Analysis
  • Vegetation & Crop Health Monitoring (NDVI)
  • Digital Elevation Model (DEM) Generation
  • Feature Extraction (Roads, Buildings)
  • Photogrammetry Services
  • Thermal Imagery Analysis

Survey & Data Collection

Accurate data is the foundation of any successful geospatial project. Our field teams are equipped with modern technology to ensure high-quality data acquisition.

  • High-Precision GPS/GNSS Surveys
  • UAV/Drone-based Mapping & Surveys
  • Topographic & Hydrographic Surveys
  • Mobile Data Collection App Development
  • Field Data Verification & Ground Truthing
  • Socio-Economic & Environmental Surveys

Web GIS & Custom Development

We build intuitive, web-based platforms that make your spatial data accessible to everyone, from stakeholders to the public, on any device.

  • Interactive Web Map Development
  • Custom GIS Dashboard Design
  • Geospatial Application Development
  • Cloud-based GIS Platform Setup
  • API Integration & Development
  • Data Visualization & Storytelling
